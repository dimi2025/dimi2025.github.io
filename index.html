About Dimitrios Spanos

Hello, I’m Dimitrios Spanos, a screenwriter with a deep passion for storytelling. My debut work, *Horizon Absolute*, is a speculative sci-fi screenplay that explores themes of technology, time travel, and the unknown. Though I am early in my career, I am dedicated to honing my craft and exploring new worlds through the written word. I am currently seeking opportunities for collaboration, feedback, and future projects.
